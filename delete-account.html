Delete Your Forge Account

You can delete your Forge account directly inside the app by going to:

Profile → Account → Delete Account

You may also request account deletion without using the app by contacting us at:

atforgesupport@gmail.com

When you delete your account, Forge deletes or anonymises the personal app data connected to your account from active systems, except for limited records we may need to retain for legal, security, fraud prevention, tax, payment, dispute, backup, or app store obligations.

Deleted app data may include your account, profile information, habits, routines, goals, journal entries, bad habit data, relapse logs, progress history, AI Coach conversations, notes, tasks, notification settings, app preferences, and other personal app data connected to your account.

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be reversed.

Deleting your Forge account does not automatically cancel any active Apple App Store or Google Play subscription. You must cancel subscriptions through the platform where you purchased them.
