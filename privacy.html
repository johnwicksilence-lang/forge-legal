Forge Privacy Policy

Last updated: 26 May 2026
This Privacy Policy explains how Forge collects, uses, stores, protects, shares, and deletes your information when you use the Forge mobile app and related services.
Forge is a personal discipline and self-improvement app that helps users track habits, routines, goals, journals, bad habits, progress, AI insights, and reminders.
This Privacy Policy applies worldwide.

1. Who We Are
Forge is operated by Gabriel Merkov, based in Switzerland.
Contact email: atforgesupport@gmail.com
For privacy questions, data requests, account deletion help, or support, contact:
atforgesupport@gmail.com

2. Information We Collect
We collect information you provide directly, information generated when you use Forge, and limited technical information needed to operate and improve the app.

3. Account Information
When you create an account or sign in, we may collect:
* name
* email address
* login provider, such as Apple or Google
* profile picture, if provided by your login provider
* user ID
* account creation date
* authentication/session information
* subscription status

4. App Content You Create
Forge stores the information you enter into the app, including:
* habits
* routines
* routine phases
* tasks
* goals
* goal categories
* journal entries
* mood ratings
* energy ratings
* stress ratings
* bad habit trackers
* relapse logs
* habit notes
* progress data
* completion history
* skipped habits
* notification preferences
* app settings
* onboarding answers, if collected
Some of this information may be personal or sensitive depending on what you choose to write.
You should not use Forge to store information needed for emergency response, urgent medical care, or crisis support.

5. AI Coach and AI Insights Data
When you use AI Coach or AI Insights, Forge may process:
* your AI messages
* relevant habit data
* routine data
* journal entries
* goal data
* bad habit and relapse data
* mood, energy, and stress ratings
* progress scores
* completion history
* AI-generated responses
* saved AI insights or conversation history
This data is used to provide personalised coaching, analysis, and insights.
AI responses are generated automatically and may be inaccurate or incomplete.

6. Subscription and Purchase Information
If you purchase Forge Premium, payments may be handled by Apple App Store, Google Play, RevenueCat, or another authorised provider.
Forge may receive limited purchase information such as:
* subscription status
* product purchased
* renewal status
* expiration date
* purchase platform
* receipt validation status
* trial status
* cancellation status
Forge does not directly collect or store your full payment card details.

7. Device and Technical Information
We may collect technical information needed to operate, secure, and improve Forge, such as:
* device type
* operating system
* app version
* crash reports
* error logs
* performance logs
* approximate region or country
* device language
* authentication/session data
* notification token, if notifications are enabled

8. Notifications
If you enable notifications, Forge may process:
* notification permissions
* notification preferences
* reminder settings
* routine reminders
* journal reminders
* task reminders
* goal deadline reminders
* quiet hours
* daily notification cap
* device notification token
Notifications may remind you about routines, tasks, journals, goals, habits, or missed activity.
You can disable notifications in the app or through your device settings.

9. How We Use Your Information
We use your information to:
* create and manage your account
* provide Forge’s core features
* save and sync your data
* track habits, routines, goals, journals, and bad habits
* calculate progress and analytics
* provide AI Coach and AI Insights
* personalise your experience
* send reminders and notifications
* manage premium access
* validate subscriptions
* provide customer support
* improve app performance
* fix bugs
* detect abuse or security issues
* comply with legal and app store obligations

10. Legal Basis for Processing
Where required by law, such as under Swiss data protection law, GDPR, or UK GDPR, we rely on the following legal bases.
Contract
We process data needed to provide Forge to you, including account data, app content, progress tracking, syncing, and subscription access.
Consent
We may rely on consent for optional features such as notifications, certain analytics, marketing messages, or AI-related processing where required.
Legitimate Interests
We may process limited data to secure Forge, prevent fraud, fix bugs, understand app performance, and improve the service.
Legal Obligations
We may process data where required for tax, legal, payment, app store, security, or regulatory reasons.

11. AI Transparency
Forge uses AI-powered features to generate coaching, insights, suggestions, and analysis.
The AI may analyse information such as habits, routines, goals, journal entries, progress, mood, energy, stress, and relapse data when you use AI features.
AI-generated responses:
* may be inaccurate
* may be incomplete
* may not fully understand your situation
* should not be treated as medical, psychological, therapeutic, legal, financial, or professional advice
Forge is not a crisis support service.
If you are in danger or need urgent help, contact emergency services or a qualified professional.

12. AI Providers and Model Training
Forge’s AI Coach and AI Insights may be powered by third-party AI providers, including Anthropic’s Claude.
When you use AI Coach or AI Insights, relevant app content may be processed by those AI providers to generate responses. This may include your AI messages, habits, routines, goals, journal entries, progress data, mood ratings, energy ratings, stress ratings, bad habit data, relapse logs, and other information needed to provide the AI feature.
We do not use your journal entries, relapse logs, AI Coach conversations, or private app content to train Forge’s own AI models.
Third-party AI providers may process data according to their own terms, privacy policies, data processing terms, and retention practices. Where available and applicable, we use provider settings or arrangements intended to limit training and retention of user content.
Forge team members do not routinely read your private content. Limited access may occur only when necessary to provide support, investigate abuse or security issues, comply with law, or fix technical problems.

13. Sharing Your Information
We do not sell your personal data.
We may share data with trusted service providers only when needed to operate Forge.
These may include:
* cloud hosting providers
* database providers
* authentication providers
* Apple App Store
* Google Play Store
* RevenueCat or another subscription provider
* Emergent AI
* Anthropic/Claude
* AI service providers
* analytics providers
* crash reporting providers
* notification providers
* customer support providers
We require service providers to process personal data only as needed to provide services to Forge and according to contractual, privacy, and security obligations.

14. International Data Transfers
Forge is intended to be available worldwide.
Your data may be processed in countries other than your country of residence, including Switzerland, the United States, the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or other countries where our providers operate.
Where required, we use appropriate safeguards for international data transfers, such as contractual protections or recognised legal transfer mechanisms.

15. Data Retention
We keep your data for as long as needed to provide Forge and maintain your account.
In general:
* account data is kept while your account exists
* app content is kept until you delete it or delete your account
* AI Coach conversations and saved AI insights are kept while your account exists, unless deleted earlier
* subscription records may be kept as long as needed for payment, tax, accounting, fraud prevention, dispute, and app store obligations
* technical logs may be kept for a limited period for security, debugging, and reliability
* backup copies may remain for a limited period before being overwritten or deleted
We may retain certain information for longer if required for:
* legal obligations
* tax records
* payment records
* fraud prevention
* security
* dispute resolution
* app store requirements
* backup recovery
When you delete your account, your personal app data is deleted or anonymised from active systems, except for limited records we may be required to keep.
Backup copies may take longer to fully expire.

16. Account Deletion
You can delete your Forge account directly inside the app by going to:
Profile → Account → Delete Account
You may also request account deletion without using the app by visiting:
https://forgesupportgrow.github.io/forge-legal/delete-account.html
When you delete your account, Forge deletes or anonymises the personal app data connected to your account from active systems, except for limited records we may need to retain for legal, security, fraud prevention, tax, payment, dispute, backup, or app store obligations.
Deleted app data may include:
* your account
* profile information
* habits
* routines
* goals
* journal entries
* bad habit trackers
* relapse logs
* progress history
* AI Coach conversations or saved AI insights
* notes
* tasks
* notification settings
* app preferences
* any other personal app data connected to your account
Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.
If you cannot access your account and need help with deletion, contact us at:
atforgesupport@gmail.com
Deleting your Forge account does not automatically cancel an Apple App Store or Google Play subscription. You must cancel subscriptions through the platform where you purchased them.
